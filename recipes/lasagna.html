Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an ancient dish, with an impressive history. The Romans used to eat it all the time at banquets , due to its delicious taste. Be sure to try this delicious dish and learn how to prepare it with us.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of pasta for lasagna
  • Bolognese sauce
  • 300 grams of mozzarella cheese
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 cups of evaporated milk
  • salt, pepper and nutmeg
  • Parmesan cheese to taste

Steps

  1. Cook for 4 minutes, one by one, the pasta sheets in salted water and two tablespoons of oil.
  2. Dip the slices in a bowl of cold water, then pat them dry one by one.
  3. To make the bechamel sauce, melt the butter and add the flour. Then add the hot milk little by little, add salt, pepper, nutmeg and Parmesan cheese to taste. Let thicken.
  4. Butter a mold and arrange a layer of dough, white sauce, and mozzarella in pieces.
  5. Alternate with another layer of masa and meat sauce. So on until finished with mozzarella cheese and drizzled with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with silver foil and bake at 350 ° F (180 ° C) for 20 minutes. Then take out the silver paper and let it gratin for approximately 5 minutes.
